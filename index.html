About Tushar Sharma

Tushar Sharma is The Happy Cold Caller, a cold calling trainer and outbound consultant from India. He helps founders and sales teams improve cold calls, discovery conversations, and meeting generation.

I believe cold calling works best when it sounds human, honest, and comfortable instead of forced, robotic, or over-scripted.

My work focuses on improving the quality of sales conversations, not just increasing activity. That means better openers, better handling of resistance, better discovery, and better next steps.

If you’re looking for practical cold calling help for your team or business, this is where the conversation starts.
